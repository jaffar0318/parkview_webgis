About Park View City

Park View City Islamabad

Park View City is a CDA-approved, beautiful residential project located in the heart of Islamabad. Surrounded by the lush green hills of Bani Gala, it offers a peaceful, family-friendly environment with modern infrastructure, commercial zones, and all basic facilities. It is developed by Vision Group and is considered one of the top housing societies in the capital.

About IGEO

Institute of Geo-Informatics & Earth Observation

The Institute of Geo-Informatics & Earth Observation (IGEO) is a hub for advanced geospatial research and education. It offers MS and PhD programs focused on GIS, Remote Sensing, and Earth Observation with applications in urban planning, disaster management, and environmental monitoring.

Location

Park View City Islamabad is situated in Malot, a rural area within Zone IV of the federal capital. It's located on Malot Road, near Bahria Enclave, and is accessible via roads like Malot Road, Kuri Dam Road, and Islamabad Highway. The project is also close to the Simly and Kuri Dam roads, a 5000-Kanal Botanical Garden, and Serena Hotel.
